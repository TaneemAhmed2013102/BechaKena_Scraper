Project Overview

ATS Dolce is a residential project by ATS group located in Sector Zeta, Greater Noida. This offers 3 BHK & 4 BHK apartments with various luxury amenities to facilitate the needs of the residents. Size of 3BHK flats varies from 1500 Sqft to 2315 sqft and size of 4BHK Apartment is 2800 Sqft.
 
The total project is constructed over 14.5 Acres of land, having 13 number of towers with 30 floors each with 1322 number of units. It is situated in one of the fastest growing area of Noida that offers easy connectivity to various prime areas. It is a ready to move in project.

Location Highlights:

ATS Dolce is located in Zeta 1 Greater Noida, a crowded enclave right in the midst of the best educational, corporate institutions and sports facilities in Noida.

Knowledge Park and the Taj Yamuna Expressway conveniently located within a short radius, ATS Dolce is set to become one of the most elite addresses in Greater Noida.


Builder Overview

ATS Infrastructure Ltd. is a renowned brand name in real estate industry across the country. ATS has 16 years of experience in the construction business and has developed 160 residential projects throughout the country. Projects developed by ATS are known for quality and reliability. This real estate giant also offers in-house project facility management, maintenance, and security services. On-going projects include ATS Casa Espana, Le Grandiose, Marigold, Triumph, Dolce, Tourmaline, Tangerine, Almond, etc. Completed projects by ATS are Pristine, Advantage, One Hamlet, Greens Village, Heavenly Foothills, etc.
